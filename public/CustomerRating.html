How Do I Leave a Review?

After a task has been invoiced by the Service Provider, you can leave a rating and review of your Service Provider in the Being Technology app or send us an Email at BeingLife.Client@gmail.com. 

Once your Service Provider finishes your task, you'll get an invoice via email detailing the number of hours worked and the total cost of the task. You can leave a rating and review for your Service Provider in that email.

When leaving a review, you can rate your experience with your Service Provider on a scale of 1 to 5 stars. 1-2 stars will display as a “negative” rating, and 3-5 stars as a “positive” rating. At the bottom of the screen, you can also leave a public written review for your Service Provider.

Review Guidelines

· Be honest, factual and focus on the performance of the task as opposed to commenting on the character of another User.

· Do not post inappropriate content (including but not limited to profanity, personal insults, slurs, threats, harassment, lewdness, hate speech or bigotry), or post anything else that is in violation of our Terms of Service.

· Do not forfeit a payment / accept a reduced payment in exchange for a positive review

· Only post about the performance of the task itself, and not your happiness or lack thereof with the platform (any comments about the platform itself should be sent to BeingLife.Client@gmail.com).

· Post in a timely manner (within 30 days of task completion) and only things that are relevant to how the task was performed.

· Only post one review per task..

· Please don’t violate other User’s privacy by posting personal information about a User.

To encourage candid feedback and avoid conflict, we ask Users to not contact each other about a negative review. If a Service Provider contacts you after you leave a review, please notify BeingLife.Client@gmail.com.
